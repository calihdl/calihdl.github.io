Why do headlights fade?

 

Years of exposure to the sun's UV rays cause headlights to oxidize and the factory UV blocking layer to degrade. This process causes yellowing of headlights.

Dim headlights are a safety hazard and can decrease the distance you see in front of you by up to 80%

Our solution

 

Our unique process removes the faded, oxidized layer and professionally re-applies a new UV blocking layer without disassembling your headlights

Our UV blocking coating is NOT a wax that you apply every month. It is a one-time coating process that restores your headlights back to almost-new condition that lasts for years
